Sustainable Fashion

Sustainable fashion focuses on creating clothing and accessories responsibly, using eco-friendly materials, reducing waste, and respecting labor practices. The goal is to make fashion environmentally and socially responsible.

Eco-Friendly Materials

Designers use organic cotton, recycled fabrics, plant-based dyes, and biodegradable materials to reduce environmental impact.

Green Jobs in Fashion

  • Sustainable Fashion Designer
  • Textile Recycling Expert
  • Bio-Couture Engineer
  • Eco-Merchandising Strategist
  • Green Supply Chain Manager

Circular Fashion Practices

Circular fashion focuses on reusing, recycling, and upcycling materials to extend the life cycle of garments and reduce textile waste in landfills.
